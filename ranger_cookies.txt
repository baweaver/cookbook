Ranger Cookies
==============
makes 4 dozen

.5C butter
.5C ea. white & brown sugars
1t vanilla
1 egg
1.25C flour
.5t ea. baking {powder,soda}
.25t salt
2C rice krispies
.25C coconut
.5C raisins|dates|nuts|chips|etc.

beat butter for 30 seconds, then add next 4 ingredients. Cream well. Sift
together next 4 ingredients and add to creamed mixture. Stir in final 3
ingredients. Drop 2" apart on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 6-10 min @ 375F.
