Potato Salad

peel, cube and cook (microwave or boil) 4 medium potatoes till tender, but not
mushy. Marinate in Italian salad dressing overnight. Meanwhile, dice 2 hard-
boiled eggs. Mix in 2T each pickle relish and chopped onion. Mix in diced
potatoes and add Italian dressing until reaching the desired consistency.
Sprinkle on some sugar if it's too vinegary.
