Butter Pecan Turtle Bars

Oven: 350 F.

Crust
-----
2c flour
1c brown sugar, packed
.5c butter, softened

Caramel Layer
-------------
2/3c butter
.5c brown sugar, packed
1c pecan halves (or chopped)
1c semi-sweet chocolate chips

Mix crust ingredients in 3qt bowl on medium speed, scraping periodically. Pat
firmly into greased 9x13 pan. Sprinkle pecans over unbaked crust.

For caramel layer, combine brown sugar and butter in 1qt saucepan over medium
heat, stirring constantly until boiling. Boil 30-60 seconds, stirring
constantly.

Pour caramel mixture evenly over crust. Bake at 350 degrees for 18-22 minutes
or until entire caramel layer is bubbly and crust is light golden brown.
Remove from oven and sprinkle with chocolate chips. Allow chips to melt (2-3
minutes) and swirl lightly with a knife (do not spread!). Cool completely at
room temperature (if chilled, layers will separate) and cut into small bars.
Makes 3-4 dozen.
