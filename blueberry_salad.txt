Blueberry Salad (serves 8-10)

1 envelope of dream whip, prepared
1 c. blueberries, drained
1 8 oz. can crushed pineapple, drained
.5 c. chopped walnuts
1 c. marshmallows
1 sm. pkg. raspberry jello

Drain pineapple and blueberries, reserving juice. Mix jello with 1 c. boiling
water, 1/3 c. pineapple juice and 2/3 c. blueberry juice.  Chill until
syrupy. Fold in dream whip, nuts, fruit and marshmallows. Chill until firm.
