Shrimp Creole (makes 2 generous servings)
=============
.75 c. chopped onion (1 med.)
1 clove garlic, minced or pressed
1 med. green pepper, finely chopped
.5 c. finely chopped celery
2 T. margarine or butter
1 can (8 oz.) tomato sauce
.5 c. water
1 bay leaf, crushed
1 t. minced fresh parsley (or .5 t. dried parsley)
.5 t. salt
.125 (1/8) t. cayenne pepper (add'l to taste)
1 7 oz. pkg. frozen shrimp, thawed (fresh tastes better :))

In a medium skillet, saute onion, garlic, green pepper and celery in butter
about 5 minutes or until tender. Remove from heat; stir in tomato sauce,
water, bay leaf, parsley, salt and cayenne pepper. Simmer 10 minutes; add
additional water if it's too thick. Add thawed shrimp and bring mixture to a
boil. Reduce heat to medium and cook five minutes. Serve over rice.
