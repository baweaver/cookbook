1 lb. polska kielbasa, sausage or hard salami
1 c. sliced carrots
.5 c. chopped onion
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
2.5 t. salt
1 t. marjoram
.5 t. thyme
.25 t. ground white pepper
1 bay leaf
1 lb. fresh mushrooms, rinsed, drained and sliced
3 c. thinly sliced cabbage
2 c. diced potato
water

Combine ingredients, add water to just cover, and simmer until vegetables are
tender (60+ minutes).
