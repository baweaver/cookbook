Cheeseburger Chowder
--------------------
1 lb. ground beef
.5c celery, finely chopped
.5c onion, chopped
3T flour
.5t salt
4c milk
1T beef boullion granules
1c (4oz.) shredded cheddar (Velveeta melts better)

Brown beef in large skillet and drain. add celery and onion and cook until
tender. Blend in flour and salt, then stir in milk and boullion. Cook and
stir over low heat till thick and bubbly. Add cheese and stir until cheese
is completely melted. 349 calories per serving; serves 4.
