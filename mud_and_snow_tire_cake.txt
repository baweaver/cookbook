1 chocolate cake mix, prepared according to package directions.
1 jar caramel ice cream topping
1 jar chocolate ice cream topping
Cool Whip
3 Heath (or Skor) bars

Bake cake as directed. Poke holes in cake with a large fork while still hot.
Pour into ice cream toppings into holes. Let cool. Frost with Cool Whip and
sprinkle chopped candy bars over the top. Keep chilled.
