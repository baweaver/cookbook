1 C butter
1 t vanilla
4 eggs
1 t lemon juice
1.5 C sugar
1 t salt
3 C flour
1.5 C chopped nuts
0.5 t baking soda dissolved in 0.5 C sour cream
1 C strawberry preserves
1 T red food coloring

blend butter, sugar, vanilla, salt and lemon juice. Beat in eggs, one at a
time. Stir in the soda/sour cream mixture. Fold in flour, nuts, preserves and
food coloring. Pour into 2 large greased and floured loaf pans.

Bake at 350 degrees for 45-60 minutes or until bread pulls away from sides
and a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let set in pan on a rack for 10
minutes, then remove from pan to finish cooling.
