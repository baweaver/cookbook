Oatmeal Cookies
===============
1C butter
1C ea. white and brown sugars
1t vanilla
2 eggs
1t ea. salt/baking soda/cinnamon
1.5C ea. oats/nuts/raisins

cream first 5 ingredients. Add next 3 ingredients. Add last 3 ingredients,
sifted together. Drop by teaspoonfuls and flatten. Bake @ 350F for 5-10
minutes.
