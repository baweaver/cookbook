Congo Bar Cookies
=================
2/3C melted butter
2C brown sugar
3 eggs
2.75C flour
2.5t baking powder
.5t salt
1t vanilla
1C nuts|coconut|dates|choc chips|  raisins

Mix in order listed, spread on cookie sheet, bake 20 min. @350 (longer for
non-stick or dark pans). Cut into bars while still warm.
