German Potato Salad
----
4 med potatoes
6 strips bacon
.5c onion, chopped
2T flour
.33c sugar
.5t celery seeds
1.5t salt
dash pepper
.5c water
.25c vinegar (more to taste)

Cook and crumble bacon. Reserve 2T drippings to cook onion in. Stir flour,
sugar and seasonings into bacon grease and onion till smooth. Then add water
and vinegar and boil till it thickens. Add potatoes and bacon.
