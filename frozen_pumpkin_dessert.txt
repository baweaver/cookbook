Frozen Pumpkin Dessert (9x13 pan)
----------------------

Crust
-----
2 c. quick rolled oats
1 c. brown sugar
2/3 c. melted butter (not margarine)
.5 c. slivered almonds

Filling
-------
1 t. cinnamon
.5 g soft vanilla ice cream
1 c. pumpkin (.5 can)
1 c. brown sugar


Toast oats in shallow pan at 350 degrees for five minutes.
Add almonds, toast 5 minutes longer. Combine with sugar/butter,
mix well and press into bottom of 9x13 pan. Leave .5 c to sprinkle
on top. Chill.

Mix all filling ingredients, pour over crust. Sprinkle with saved
crust mixture, freeze till hard.
